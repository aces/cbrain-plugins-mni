CIVET Directories Decrypted


A CIVET processing job typically requires two names (or identifiers) to be optionally specified before the job starts, the default naming conventions are shown below.
  • {prefix}_{dsid}_t1.mnc
    • t1 could be replaced by t2 or pd
    • dsid is the unique subject name
    • prefix is the name of the study itself

native: images in native space
  • original t1 image
    • prefix_id_t1.mnc
  • t1 image partially corrected for non-uniformities
    • prefix_id_t1_nuc.mnc
final: images in stereotaxic space
  • original t1 image transformed in stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_t1_tal.mnc
  • original t1 image transformed in stereotaxic space, corrected for non-uniformities
    • prefix_id_t1_final.mnc
  • transformed non-linearly to stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_t1_nl.mnc
    • t1_final.mnc
transforms/linear: linear transformations
  • linear transformation from native to stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_t1_tal.xfm
transforms/nonlinear: non-linear transformations
  • non-linear transformation from linear stereotaxic space to stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_nlfit_It.xfm
  • deformation field for non-linear transformation
    • prefix_id_nlfit_It_grid_0.xfm
transforms/surfreg: surface transformations
  • surface maps for left/right hemispheres to surface model
    • prefix_id_left_surfmap.sm
    • prefix_id_right_surfmap.sm
mask: brain masks
  • brain mask in stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_skull_mask.mnc
  • brain mask in native space
    • prefix_id_skull_mask_native.mnc
  • brain mask, without cerebellum, in stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_brain_mask.mnc
    • prefix_id_cortex.obj
classify: classified image
  • for future usage
    • prefix_id_artefact.mnc
  • final masked discrete tissue classification in stereotaxic space after correction for partial volumes
    • prefix_id_classify.mnc
  • masked discrete tissue classification in stereotaxic space
    • prefix_id_cls_clean.mnc
  • total volume of tissue types in native space (in mm^3)
    • prefix_id_cls_volumes.dat
  • pure and mixed tissue classes
    • prefix_id_pve_disc.mnc
  • partial volume estimates for csf
    • prefix_id_pve_csf.mnc
  • partial volume estimates for gray matter
    • prefix_id_pve_gm.mnc
  • partial volume estimates for white matter
    • prefix_id_pve_wm.mnc
surfaces: surfaces in stereotaxic space
  • white matter surfaces
    • prefix_id_white_surface_left_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_white_surface_right_81920.obj
  • final white matter surfaces after t1-gradient correction
    • prefix_id_white_surface_calibrated.obj
    • prefix_id_white_surface_left_calibrated_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_white_surface_right_calibrated_81920.obj
  • resampled calibrated white surfaces
    • prefix_id_white_surface_rsl_left_calibrated_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_white_surface_rsl_right_calibrated_81920.obj
  • final gray matter (pial) surface
    • prefix_id_gray_surface.obj
    • prefix_id_gray_surface_left_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_gray_surface_right_81920.obj
  • resampled gray matter surfaces
    • prefix_id_gray_surface_rsl_left_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_gray_surface_rsl_right_81920.obj
  • final mid surfaces (between white and gray)
    • prefix_id_mid_surface.obj
    • prefix_id_mid_surface_left_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_mid_surface_right_81920.obj
  • resampled mid surfaces
    • prefix_id_mid_surface_rsl_left_81920.obj
    • prefix_id_mid_surface_rsl_right_81920.obj
  • gyrification index for whole brain or hemispheres
    • prefix_id_gi.dat
    • prefix_id_gi_left.dat
    • prefix_id_gi_right.dat
  • sulcal depth potential map for surface registration
    • prefix_id_left_dataterm.vv
    • prefix_id_right_dataterm.vv
thickness: cortical thickness maps
  • Volume of cortex in native space
    • prefix_id_cerebral_volume.txt
  • mean surface curvature, blurred at 20mm
    • prefix_id_native_mc_20mm.txt
    • prefix_id_native_mc_20mm_left.txt
    • prefix_id_native_mc_20mm_right.txt
    • prefix_id_native_mc_rsl_20mm_left.txt
    • prefix_id_native_mc_rsl_20mm_right.txt
  • native cortical thickness, blurred at 20mm
    • prefix_id_native_rms_tlink_20mm.txt
    • prefix_id_native_rms_tlink_20mm_left.txt
    • prefix_id_native_rms_tlink_20mm_right.txt
  • native cortical thickness, blurred at 20mm
    • prefix_id_native_rms_rsl_tlink_20mm.txt
    • prefix_id_native_rms_rsl_tlink_20mm_left.txt
    • prefix id native rms rsl tlink 20mm right.txt
  • asymmetry maps for cortical thickness (resampled left-right)
    • prefix_id_native_rms_rsl_tlink_20mm_asym.txt
    • prefix id native_rms_rsl_tlink_20mm_asym_hemi.txt
rsl = resampled on surface model, to use for group comparison analysis

Voxel-based morphometry (VBM): VBM maps
  • VBM maps for CSF, gray and white matter, blurred at 8mm
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_csf.mnc
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_gm.mnc
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_wm.mnc
  • right/left differences of blurred VBM maps for CSF, gray and white matter
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_csf_sym.mnc
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_gm_sym.mnc
    • prefix_id_smooth_8mm_wm_sym.mnc
temp: temporary working files
  • intermediate files mostly kept for advanced debugging purposes, if a failure occurs
verify: quality control images
  • quality control image for registration and classification
    • prefix_id_verify.png:
  • quality control image for surface extraction
    • prefix_id_clasp.png
  • error (%) for skull mask in native space
    • prefix_id_brainmask_qc.txt
  • classified tissue percentages
    • prefix_id_classify_qc.txt
  • error (%) for white and gray surfaces
    • prefix_id_surface_qc.txt
