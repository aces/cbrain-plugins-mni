Options available for previous versions

x-step
x-voxel size in native space (mm)
y-step
y-voxel size in native space (mm)
z-step
z-voxel size in native space (mm)
NatMaskErr (only for CIVET 1.1.10 and 1.1.11)
native space brain masking error
StxMaskErr
stereotaxic space brain masking error
CSFcls
% of classified cerebral spinal fluid
GMcls
% of classified grey matter in the brain
WMcls
% of classified white matter in the brain
GMCortical (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Volume of total cortical gray matter (without sub-cortical gray)
WMsurf
% error in extraction of white surface
GMsurf
% error in expansion of grey surface
SRLeft
number of self intersections left hemisphere
SRRight
number of self intersections right hemisphere
SSLeft (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Number of surface-surface intersections between wm-gm left surfaces
SSRight (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Number of surface-surface intersections between wm-gm right surfaces
MeanCTLeft (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Mean cortical thickness for left hemispheres
MeanCTRight (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Mean cortical thickness for right hemispheres
MeanWM-T1 (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Mean T1 intensity for classified white matter
StdDevWM-T1 (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
StdDev T1 intensity for classified white matter
MeanGM-T1 (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
Mean T1 intensity for classified grey matter
StdDevGM-T1 (only for CIVET 1.1.12)
StdDev T1 intensity for classified grey matter
GIleft
Gyrification index for left hemisphere
GIright
Gyrification index for right hemisphere
GIfull
Gyrification full index for full brain
